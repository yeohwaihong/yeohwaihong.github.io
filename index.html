About Ka Bakes


Baking since 1990

Erecca, the founder of the brand, is a humble home baker with all the makings of a passionate entrepreneur. She whips up the most perfect macarons I have seen and from the feedback (of 6 foodies), the macarons tasted absolutely heavenly. I myself got to try her chocolate, salted caramel, green tea, raspberry, thai milk tea, rose, sesame, coconut and earl grey flavours. Each and every flavour was perfect. No flavour was too sweet nor did any of it taste artificial in anyway.
